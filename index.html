Welcome to the website of the Capital (London) Region of the Goldwing Owners Club of Great Britain, commonly referred to as "Capital Wings" or "The Moles" (because we pop up everywhere!).
 
We are a very active and friendly club, attending many camping events and rallys both nationally and internationally each year, and organising social "ride-outs" and visits (often involving food) during the better weather.  In addition various parties, barbecues, and other social events take place throughout the year, frequently in aid of charities.
 
If you would like to find out more about us, please contact us or come along to one of our regular club nights, where you will be very welcome.

Meetings 8pm on the 1st & 3rd Thursdays of the month at:

The Woodbine, Honey Lane, Waltham Abbey, EN9 9QT
